How GoLynk Works for Carriers

It's simple: we only show you jobs that fit the routes you already walk, ride, or drive.

1

See Jobs That Match Your Route

The GoLynk app understands your regular routes, time windows, and vehicle type. It only shows deliveries that fit your natural path—no detours, no random jobs across the city.

2

Accept What Fits Your Day

You choose which tasks to accept based on your schedule and capacity. No one forces you to take every job, and you can pause or stop anytime.

3

Deliver On-Route, Get Paid Fast

You deliver parcels along your usual route and mark them completed in the app. Payouts are processed automatically, with a clear breakdown of what you earned for each trip.
